About Us

Welcome to the Photographers Association Kanpur. Photographer Association Kanpur has been actively working for the growth and development of photographers over the past five years. Our journey is dedicated to building a strong creative community where both beginners and professionals can learn, share, and grow together. Through regular workshops, practical training sessions, photo walks, and expert guidance programs, we have helped photographers improve their technical skills as well as their artistic vision. We also focus on modern photography trends, editing techniques, and business development skills so that members can build successful careers. Over the years, we have created a supportive platform that encourages creativity, networking, and continuous learning. Our association remains committed to empowering photographers in Kanpur by providing opportunities, exposure, and mentorship to help them achieve excellence in the field of photography.

Our Activities

We organize photography workshops, exhibitions, photo walks, and training sessions to improve skills and creativity among members.
